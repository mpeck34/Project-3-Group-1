London Bike Share Analysis

Project purpose: Our purpose is to create a healthier and environmentally-friendly city that supports bike use as a primary form of transport for residents and tourists

Project objective: Provide data that has the objective to inform city built environment strategies and inform how to supply bikes to meet demand.

Project analysis:
- What additional infrastructure (bike stations, paths) is needed to make the bike project successful;
- How to ensure supply of bikes meet demand (what point in time, i.e time of day or day of week, are bikes required the most) - ensure operational smoothness for the city supply of bikes.
